Holistic Nutrition

Holistic Nutrition takes into account an individual’s unique needs, lifestyle, and environment to create a personalized yet healthy approach to eating. It emphasizes whole, unprocessed foods that are nutrient-dense and minimally altered, such as fresh fruits and vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

It goes beyond focusing solely on a individual calorie intake. The principles of holistic nutrition revolve around the belief that optimal health is achieved by nurturing and balancing all aspects of an individual’s well-being

It plays a significant role in mind-body-spirit connection and recognises that all aspects of a person are interconnected to one another. It acknowledges that our thoughts, emotions, and spiritual well-being can greatly impact our physical health and vice versa and the fact that the food we eat can also affect us both mentally and physically.

By addressing mind-body-spirit connection, it can bring tons of benefits including reducing stress, promoting emotional and spirituality well-being, self-love, self-care and self-reflection. It can also reduce your cravings, stabilise your moods and improve your sleep quality.

To apply the Holistic approach is to cut down animal products to 15% of your diet and increase plant-based products up to 85%. This is because animal products are unnecessary for most people. However, you do need to take your protein intake into account
